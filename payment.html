FAQ

Good questions, clear answers.

Are the classes really live? +

Yes. Every session is conducted live online with a real teacher — no pre-recorded videos. You join at the scheduled time and learn in real time.

What platform are the classes on? +

Classes are held live over Zoom, with Google Meet used occasionally. Your class group stays connected through a Facebook Messenger group chat, where we share schedules, materials, and reminders. You'll get the links and group invite once you're enrolled.

Do I need to know any Spanish first? +

Not at all. Our Foundations Program is designed for complete beginners with no prior Spanish required. We start from the very basics.

How do I reserve a slot? +

Fill out the form below or message us on Facebook, Viber, or WhatsApp. For the Foundations Program you can reserve your seat for ₱599 and pay the remaining balance before the start date.

What payment options are available? +

We accept GoTyme Bank, BPI, MetroBank, and Wise, with both installment and cash options. Once you reserve, we'll send the exact payment details and walk you through the plan that works best for you.

How does the ₱599 reservation work? +

The ₱599 reserves your slot and is credited toward your program fee — you simply pay the remaining balance before your start date. Please note the reservation is non-refundable, so reserve once you're ready to commit.

Do I get a certificate? +

Yes. You'll receive a certificate of completion after finishing your program — a great addition to your CV and LinkedIn.

Where are you based? +

We're based in Cebu, Philippines and registered with the DTI (National). Classes are 100% online, so you can join from anywhere.

Can the 1-on-1 schedule fit my shift? +

Yes — the private program is fully flexible and personalized. We build the schedule around your routine. Just tell us your availability.

Privacy Policy

El Fuego Academy PH · Last updated June 2026

Only the details you give us — your name, email, mobile number, program of interest, schedule preference, and anything you write in your message or send us on Facebook, Viber, or WhatsApp.

How we use it

To reply to you, share class schedules and materials, arrange payments, and deliver our Spanish programs. We may store your details in the customer-management tools we use to run the school.
